Remote Environment

With the application of wireless humidity and temperature control we are able to remotely manage the environmental parameters of the air column inside the closed grow box stack.

Energy Saving

Our system uses very low energy as the grow stacks are completely insulated, mitigating heat loss inside a closed vertically stacked modular structure with full control over ingress and egress of fresh and stale air.
